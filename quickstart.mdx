## Sign up or sign in

1. Go to [EduTube](https://vashishta.d12rp6k6j03qru.amplifyapp.com)
2. Click **Create account** or **Sign in**
3. Sign up with:
   - Email
   - Username
   - Password
4. Sign in with your email and password

After signing in, you land on your **Home** dashboard.

## Create your first journey

You can create a journey in two ways:

### Option A: From a YouTube playlist

1. On Home, click **Create New Journey**
2. Choose **Import from YouTube playlist**
3. Paste a YouTube playlist URL (e.g. `https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL...`)
4. Toggle **Public** if you want others to see it
5. Click **Create from playlist**

EduTube imports all videos as chapters automatically.

### Option B: Custom journey

1. On Home, click **Create New Journey**
2. Choose **Create Custom Journey**
3. Enter:
   - Journey name
   - Description (optional)
4. Toggle **Public** if you want to share
5. Click **Create**

You’ll start with an empty journey; add chapters manually later.

## Open a journey and watch

1. On Home, click a journey row to open it
2. You’ll see the **Chapters** list
3. Click a chapter title to open the video player
4. Use **Mark as complete** when done
5. Use **Notes** next to a chapter to add notes

## Explore and fork

1. Click **Explore** in the nav
2. Browse public journeys
3. Click **Fork** to copy a journey to your account
4. Use **Open notes** to view the journey’s notes
