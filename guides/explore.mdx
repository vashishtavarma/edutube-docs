## What is Explore?

**Explore** shows public journeys shared by the community. You can browse them, read descriptions, and copy any journey to your account.

## Browsing journeys

1. Click **Explore** in the top navigation
2. Use the search box to filter by name
3. Each row shows:
   - **Journey name**
   - **Owner** (username)
   - **Description**
   - **Notes** link
   - **Actions** (Fork / Yours)

## Forking a journey

**Fork** creates a copy of a journey in your account.

1. On Explore, find a journey you like
2. Click **Fork**
3. The journey is copied to your **Home** as your own journey

You can then:

- Edit chapters and notes
- Change visibility
- Delete it

The original owner’s journey is unchanged.

## "Yours" label

If a journey is yours (you created it), you’ll see **Yours** instead of **Fork**. Click the row to open it as usual.

## Opening notes

Use **Open notes** to view notes for any public journey. You can read them without forking. Your own notes are only for journeys you’ve forked or created.
