## What are notes?

Notes are saved per chapter. You can add multiple notes to one chapter. Notes have a **title** and **content**.

## Viewing notes

### Notes for a journey

1. Open a journey
2. Click **Notes** (top of the page)

You’ll see all notes for that journey, grouped by chapter.

### Notes for a chapter

1. Open a journey
2. Click **Notes** next to a specific chapter

You’ll go to the notes view scoped to that chapter.

### From Home or Explore

- On Home: Click **Open notes** in the Notes column
- On Explore: Click **Open notes** for any journey

## Adding a note

1. Open a journey and go to a chapter’s video player
2. Use the notes panel or **Notes** link
3. Add a new note with:
   - **Title** (optional) — e.g. “Key formulas”
   - **Content** — Your text
4. Save

Notes are linked to the chapter and journey.

## Editing a note

1. Open the notes page for the journey or chapter
2. Click the note you want to edit
3. Change the title or content
4. Save

## Deleting a note

1. Open the notes page
2. Open the note
3. Use the delete action
4. Confirm

## Tips

- Use titles to group related ideas (e.g. “Definitions”, “Summary”)
- Notes are stored per chapter, so they stay with the content
- You can open and edit notes from the journey page or the video player
