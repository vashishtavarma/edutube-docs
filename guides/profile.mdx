## Opening your profile

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Choose **Dashboard**

Or go to `/profile` when signed in.

## What you’ll see

### Profile header

- Your **username** and **email**
- Avatar with your initials

### Stats

- **Total journeys** — Number of journeys you have
- **Average progress** — Mean completion % across all journeys
- **Completed** — Journeys at 100% completion

### Your journeys

A list of your journeys with:

- Journey name (click to open)
- Completion percentage
- Progress bar

### Overall Journey Progress

A chart showing your overall completion across all journeys.

## How progress is calculated

- Each chapter can be marked **complete**
- Journey progress = (completed chapters / total chapters) × 100
- **Average progress** = mean of all journey progress values
- **Completed** = journeys where progress = 100%
