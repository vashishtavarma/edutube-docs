## What is the chatbot?

EduTube includes an AI assistant that can answer questions about your learning and the platform. It appears when you’re signed in.

## Opening the chatbot

1. Sign in to EduTube
2. Click the **chat** button (usually bottom right)
3. A chat panel opens

## Using the chatbot

- Ask questions in natural language
- Examples:
  - “How do I create a journey from a playlist?”
  - “Explain this chapter’s concept”
  - “What is EduTube?”
- The chatbot responds with short, focused answers

## Context

The chatbot can use your current context (for example, the journey or chapter you’re viewing) when it’s available. Some deployments may also use a knowledge base for better answers.

## Availability

The chatbot requires the backend to be configured with an AI provider (e.g. AWS Bedrock). If it’s not set up, the chat button may be hidden or show an error.
