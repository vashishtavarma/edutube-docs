## What is EduTube?

EduTube is a learning platform that turns any YouTube playlist into a structured learning journey. You can:

- **Create journeys** from YouTube playlists or build custom paths
- **Track progress** per chapter with completion status
- **Take notes** per chapter and organize your learning
- **Explore** public journeys shared by the community and fork them
- **Get help** from an AI chatbot while learning
